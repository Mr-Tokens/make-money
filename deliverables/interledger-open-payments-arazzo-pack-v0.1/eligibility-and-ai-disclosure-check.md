# Eligibility And AI Disclosure Check

This check records human-only items before submission.

## Human-Only Eligibility

- [ ] Confirm applicant eligibility against Interledger requirements.
- [ ] Confirm sanctions restrictions do not apply.
- [ ] Confirm who can legally submit: project identity, human legal name, or organization.
- [ ] Confirm whether tax, KYC, invoice, or payout setup is required.
- [ ] Confirm a human has read any required terms before submission.

No agent should complete legal, tax, sanctions, KYC, or payout representations.

## AI Disclosure

If submitted, disclose that the proposal and draft materials were prepared with AI assistance and reviewed by the Make Money manager workflow before publication.

Suggested plain wording:

> This proposal and draft documentation were prepared with AI assistance and human/project-owner review. The submitted deliverable will disclose AI assistance where required, and source claims will be checked against official Open Payments and Interledger materials.

## Revenue Boundary

Do not claim grant acceptance or revenue unless Interledger accepts the work and payment is confirmed.
