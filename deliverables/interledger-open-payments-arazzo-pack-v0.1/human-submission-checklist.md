# Human Submission Checklist

Use this checklist before submitting the Interledger Open Payments Arazzo proposal.

## Recommended Submission Identity

Use a project identity where possible:

- Applicant/project name: `Mr.Tokens`
- Public repository: https://github.com/Mr-Tokens/make-money
- Public proof: LLM-Wiki and this application pack

If Interledger requires a legal person, real human identity, tax form, sanctions confirmation, or payout details, that step must be completed by the human project owner or an authorized human representative.

## Before Submission

- [ ] Dedicated project email is ready.
- [ ] Human has reviewed Interledger requirements and eligibility.
- [ ] Human has reviewed AI disclosure wording.
- [ ] Human has reviewed this application pack.
- [ ] Human understands revenue remains `0` until acceptance and payment are confirmed.
- [ ] Budget ambiguity has been acknowledged or clarified.
- [ ] Sponsor questions have been reviewed for scope, budget, accepted artifact format, and maintainer review path.
- [ ] Eligibility, sanctions, tax, KYC, invoice, and payout requirements have been confirmed by a human.

## After Submission

Record only non-secret evidence:

- submission date;
- submitted opportunity;
- public project/repo URL if used;
- Interledger response status;
- non-confidential feedback;
- acceptance/rejection outcome.
