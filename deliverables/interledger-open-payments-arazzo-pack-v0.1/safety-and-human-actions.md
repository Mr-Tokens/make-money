# Safety And Human Actions

## Safety Boundaries

- No automated system submits the grant application.
- No automated system agrees to Interledger terms.
- No automated system completes sanctions, tax, KYC, identity, or payout fields.
- No wallet is connected.
- No payment is initiated.
- No test wallet credentials are stored in tracked files.
- No access tokens, client keys, or private keys are requested or recorded.
- No revenue is recorded until acceptance and payment are confirmed.

## Human-Only Actions

- Confirm eligibility and sanctions constraints.
- Review grant terms and application requirements.
- Submit the application if desired.
- Provide legal name, email, organization, CV, tax, or payout details if required.
- Complete any sponsor follow-up.
- Decide whether a public sponsor-feedback discussion should be posted before applying.

## AI Disclosure

The Interledger SDK grant wiki permits generative AI use with disclosure and requires review of AI-generated output. Any application should disclose:

- AI assistance was used;
- Codex reviewed and edited worker/model output;
- final responsibility remains with the human applicant or authorized project representative.

## Risk Notes

- The proposal may be rejected.
- Budget interpretation needs sponsor clarification.
- Documentation may need maintainer review before it can be useful upstream.
- Live examples can introduce credential and payment risks; avoid them until a safe test plan exists.
