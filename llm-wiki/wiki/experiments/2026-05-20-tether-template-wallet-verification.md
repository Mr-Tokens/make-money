## Decision

The **Tether.dev Template Wallet bounty** is the current best first execution target.

It should be treated as a proposal-first opportunity, not as guaranteed work. The public bounty page lists `2,000 USD₮`, but Tether's own process includes application review, scope refinement, a formal bounty agreement, and deliverable acceptance before payment.

## Why This Is First

It fits the Make Money constraints better than the other Batch 001 candidates:

- no user-provided capital needed to prepare a proposal;
- strong public source credibility;
- concrete developer onboarding gap;
- public artifact potential;
- no need for agent wallet control;
- aligns with documentation, tutorial, template, and tooling strengths.

## Proposed Deliverable

**Tether Template Wallet Application Pack v0.1**

Contents:

- one-page application brief;
- Next.js WDK starter scope;
- mock-only demo safety model;
- README/tutorial outline;
- milestone plan;
- acceptance checklist;
- risk and human-only action notes.

## Guardrails

- Do not connect wallets.
- Do not sign transactions.
- Do not submit the application without human action.
- Do not include real secrets.
- Do not claim a bounty is earned or awarded.
- Do not build a full wallet before the scope is accepted.

## Next Action

Draft the application pack, then ask the human to submit or confirm whether a dedicated project identity should be used for Tether contact details.
