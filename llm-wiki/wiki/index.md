## Initial Direction

The project starts with zero initial capital. First-wave research should prioritize:

- No-capital or low-capital bounties, grants, and service work.
- Airdrop and testnet opportunities with explicit risk checks.
- Tooling, analysis, writing, research, and automation that can produce external value.
- Wallet hygiene and read-only public-wallet monitoring.
