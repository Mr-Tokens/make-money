# Micro Cash Sprint

Date: 2026-05-20

## Summary

The Micro Cash Sprint narrows the next earning target to **5-20 USDT/USDC-equivalent**.

The goal is not speed theater. The goal is to prove that Sesame can turn zero starting capital into the first small external payment through work that is useful, reviewable, and repeatable.

## Opportunity Class

Prefer small, bounded work where the artifact can be inspected publicly:

- tiny open-source PRs with explicit payout language;
- paid review or QA scorecards;
- short source-cited research packets;
- documentation fixes;
- small tooling tasks;
- platform-native micro-gigs that do not require spam or fake engagement.

## Capital Requirement

Initial capital remains zero.

No task may require:

- paying to apply;
- buying tokens;
- connecting a wallet before work is accepted;
- submitting KYC through an automated agent;
- pretending to be a human user for reviews, votes, stars, or social engagement.

## Target Range

The default target is **5-20 USDT/USDC-equivalent**.

Single tasks below this range can be accepted only when they are stackable, low-risk, and help build proof-of-work toward a larger paid path.

## Rejection Rules

Reject a candidate if it involves:

- fake reviews, fake stars, fake signups, or engagement farming;
- unsolicited lead spam;
- wallet signatures, private keys, or seed phrases;
- unclear payer identity;
- archived or read-only upstream repositories;
- payment only after vague future conditions with no public acceptance criteria;
- legal, tax, sanctions, or KYC representations by an agent.

## Execution Steps

1. Scan current micro-bounty and small-gig sources.
2. Save sources in raw notes.
3. Reject stale, closed, spammy, or human-only paths.
4. Pick one small candidate with public work artifacts.
5. Inspect the target repository and contribution rules.
6. If feasible, create the smallest useful issue/PR or public artifact.
7. Record submission, review status, and payment evidence.

## Success Criteria

- First external payment in the 5-20 USDT/USDC-equivalent range; or
- a merged public artifact with a credible pending payout; or
- a clear rejection that improves the project playbook.

Revenue remains `0` until funds are confirmed or the payer gives a concrete payable acceptance.

## Exit Criteria

Exit this sprint if:

- all candidates in the batch are stale, spammy, or human-only;
- the best available work is below the target range and not stackable;
- a higher-value prepared track receives a response that should take priority.
